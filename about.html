About Us

Medland Corporation is an Indian company specializing in pharmaceutical and nutraceutical products.

It was established under the Companies Act, 2013. The registered address of the company is in New Delhi, India.

The company is dedicated to delivering high-quality health solutions and adheres to rigorous standards to ensure excellence and innovation in its offerings..
